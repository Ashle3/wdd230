Target Audience

The target audience this website is trying to reach, is business owners that are looking for opportunities to promote their business. They will be looking for advertisement that is reasonable, both financially and logistically, and will be looking to see if their local Chamber of Commerce offers both. Since they are business owners, most of them will be adults at a minimum age of 30 and up. At least in the city of Frisco, they will more than likely be married/in a relationship, and might have kids. Since they are business owners, they will have had a reasonable amount of experience running and managing businesses, and will therefore have a pretty clear picture on how they want their business advertised.

Personas

Persona 1

Mary is a small business owner who opened her bakery within the last year. Business has been going well, but she hasn't been getting as much foot traffic as she would have liked. She wants a way to advertise her store beyond just social media posts, and wants to become more involved in the community. She learned recently that the Frisco Chamber of Commerce might have the ammenities she had been looking for, and wants to investigate the Chamber more to find out exactly what it is they offer local businesses.

Persona 2

Rodrigo runs a small Italian restaurant, and is looking to expand his business, but needs a lot to put his new store at. He is very busy running his restaurant, and needs help picking out a location. He wishes there was an easy way to network with other companies to see if they knew of any available lots that were nearby. He heard of the Frisco Chamber of Commerce, but wasn't sure if he should join because it might be too expensive. Was there a way he could still join the Chamber without having to spend too much money?

Scenarios

  • How successful/organized is this Chamber of Commerce? How have other businesses benefitted from joining them?
  • What other companies have joined this Chamber of Commerce?
  • How do I join my company to this Chamber of Commerce?
  • What are the benefits of joining? Are there any downsides?
  • Who leads this Chamber of Commerce? How can I contact them?
